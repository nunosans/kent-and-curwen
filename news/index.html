Kent & Curwen Royal Charity Polo Cup

On July 21st, the Kent & Curwen Royal Charity Polo Cup was hosted at Lord Andrew Lloyd Webber’s Berskhire Estate, Watership Down. The glamourous event saw over 400 VIP guests attend to support Sentebale and Tusk Trust - Prince Harry and Prince William’s respective charities.

Special Guests included Prince Harry of Wales, Lord and Lady Lloyd Webber, Sabrina Fung, Aaron Kwok, Timothy Fok, Lady Gabriella Windsor, Lady Henrietta Spencer Churchill, and Twiggy.

Kent & Curwen Flannels For Heroes

Under sunny skies, Kent & Curwen hosted the Kent & Curwen Flannels for Heroes charity cricket match on the historical grounds of the Chelsea Royal Hospital at Burton Court, London, to benefit wounded British servicemen through the charities Help for Heroes and Walking with the Wounded.

World famous cricketers in attendance included Kevin Pietersen, Michael Vaughan, and Isa Guha.. Also lending their support to this worthwhile cause were Ben Goldsmith, Lady Frederick Windsor, HSH Princess Olga Romanoff and legendary actress Peggy Cummins, who supported the original fund raising exhibition matches of the infamous Hollywood Cricket Club in the 1940’s that dressed in Kent & Curwen’s classic cricket attire.

Kent & Curwen Centenary Sprint Cup

Kent & Curwen hosted the “Kent & Curwen Centenary Sprint Cup” celebrations at the Shatin Racecourse on January 15, 2012.

This year’s event featured an extraordinary lineup of races and entertaining programs; and included a special appearance from Guest-of-Honor, Mr. Peter Mark Andrew Phillips the eldest grandson of Queen Elizabeth II, the only son of Princess Anne, The Princess Royal, and her husband, Captain Mark Phillips.

Other prestigious guests in attendance included glitterati and horse lovers: Mr. and Mrs. Vincent and Loretta Lo, Mr. Eric Fok, Ms. Irene Wan, Mr. and Mrs. Dominique and Jane Lee, Ms. Vivienne Tam, Mr. Tommy Hilfiger, Ms. Sabrina Fung, Dr. Victor Fung, Dr. William Fung, Allan Zeman and Mr. Aaron Kwok, the Brand Ambassador for Kent & Curwen.
